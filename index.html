ABOUT HACK HSV

Hack HSV is the first ever high school hackathon in Alabama. Hack HSV is a 12 hour problem solving marathon where you will collaborate with friends to create something that the world has never seen before. You will meet new people, learn new skills, and, of course, code. Hack HSV’s mission is to spread the love of programming and innovation to everyone by making our event as accessible as possible.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A hackathon is a coding competition where students collaborate to create something based on a theme, which be announced at the competition, whether it is a web application or an IOS game.

WHEN IS IT?

The competition is on April 15, 2017 from 8:00am - 10:30pm. You can find the full schedule below.

WHERE IS IT?

Hack HSV is taking place at Randolph Cafeteria; at Randolph School in Huntsville, Alabama - 4915 Garth Rd SE, Huntsville, Alabama

WHO CAN ATTEND HACK HSV?

Anyone ages 13-18 from any part of the world can register. If you love to solve problems and want to have a great time, then you are welcome at Hack HSV.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

All you need to bring is a computer and charger to write your brilliant code.

WHAT CAN I WIN?

We have plenty of awesome prizes that will be provided by our incredible sponsors. Prizes will be given out to winners of first, second, and third place as well as to superlative categories.

CAN I TRAVEL WITH A TEAM?

You can choose to travel with a team, chose a team when you arrive, or just work alone. We have have a special time dedicated for you to find partners to work with.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

Registering along with all food, T-Shirts, and prizes are included in a cost of only $20!

I HAVE MORE QUESTIONS.

If you have anymore questions, drop us a line at hackhsv@gmail.com
