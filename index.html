FAQ's

WHAT IS A HACKATHON?

A hackathon is a coding competition where students will collaborate with friends and use code to solve a problem, that will be announced at the competition, based on the theme of space whether it is a website, an IOS app, or a game on Scratch.

WHEN IS IT?

The competition is on February 24, 2018 from 8:00am - 10:30pm.

WHERE IS IT?

Hack HSV is taking place at Randolph Commons at Randolph School in Huntsville, Alabama - 4915 Garth Rd SE, Huntsville, Alabama

WHO CAN ATTEND HACK HSV?

Anyone ages 13-18 from any part of the world can participate. Registrations will open soon!

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

All you need to bring is a computer and charger to write your brilliant code.

DO I HAVE TO KNOW HOW TO CODE?

Absolutely not! You just need to have a laptop and a smile in order to code as we have plenty of mentors that will be there to help you code from your first line to your last!

WHAT CAN I MAKE?

You will be able to make anything you want from ios apps to websites to virtual reality games. Note, that you will only be eligible to win prizes if your final product falls under the theme of the competition.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

Registering along with all food, T-Shirts, and prizes are included in a cost of only $25!

WHEN DO REGISTRATIONS OPEN?

Registrations are almost here, so be prepared!
