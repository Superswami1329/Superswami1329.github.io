ABOUT HACKHSV

HackHSV is a High School Hackathon created by High Schoolers in Huntsville, Alabama that is a part of the MLH 2017 Hackathon season. It is fun competition for a life-changing experience where you will get to meet new people, learn new things, and, of course, code.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

WHEN IS IT?

The Competition is from April 1 - 2, 2017. You can see the full schedule below.

WHERE IS IT?

The competition is located at the Shield Jones Gym at Randolph School's Garth Campus

WHO CAN ATTEND HACKHSV?

Anyone ages 13-19 can join. If you love you computer science and want to have a fun time, then you are welcome at HackHSV.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING?

All you need to bring is your laptop with charges, preferably a sleeping bag, and your creative brain.

WHAT CAN I WIN?

We have plenty of awesome prizes that are provided by our sponsors. Prizes will be given out to first, second, and thrid places as well as to superalive categories (ie most creative)

HARDWARE?

We have plenty of hardware for you to work with. Arduino, breadboards, and Oculus Rift to name a few.

WHAT ARE THE RULES?

All the rules that you have to follow are under MLH's Code of Conduct. Every rule is strickly enforeced, and any violations will not be tolerated.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?

HackHSV does not cost anything. Registering along with All Food, T-Shirts, and Prizes are free.

HAVE MORE QUESTIONS?

If you have anymore questions, you can ask our team at info@hackhsv.com
